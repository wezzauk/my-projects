Learning Mandarin

Having completed a few projects in the past few months and most of which are still ongoing. I've decided to embark on another journey, that is to learn mandarin.

I'm of chinese ethnicity but was born and raised in the United Kingdom. My first language is english and though I spoke a dialect of chinese at home with my parents, my chinese was and still is very poor. My grammar is poor, my vocbulary is limited and basically I speak like a very young child (probably worse!).

I've chosen this personal challenge to improve this situation and know it will take years and more of studying but it all has to start somewhere.

I currently know 2 words of mandarin, nǐ hǎo and xièxie. Basically I'm starting from scratch. My aim to get to some degree of fluency so I can be conversational. I would love to be able to travel around China to see the sights and sample the food over there all by speaking mandarin.
