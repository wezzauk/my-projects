Balance Acupuncture Meridian Selector App

I'm qualified acupuncturist though I do not practise anymore. I had learned a system of acupuncture called balance acupuncture. The premise was that you could insert needles into specific meridians depending on the affected meridian and get near instant results!

The approach is based on 5 systems and the meridian that needs to be treated depends on the meridian that is affected, or the 'sick' meridian. Once the sick meridian has been identified, the meridians used for treatment can be calculated according to a set of rules.

I made this app so the acupuncturist can simply identify the sick meridian and select the appropriate yellow button. The app will then display the correct meridians that can be used to treat the condition based on the set of rules for calculation.
